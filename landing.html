Welcome to SBURBSim!!!

SBURBSim is a browser simulation of a game called "SBURB", which destroys a planet and allows the survivors to attempt to achieve God hood and create a Universe. There are many things you can do!
  • You can read a random session!
    • There are 4294967296 different possible sessions!
    • Rare sessions may allow you to go back in time to alter them.
  • You can customize a random session!
    • You can alter the players in a session. Choose how they look, their claspects, their interests, even their species!
    • You can change the number of players in a session.
    • You can save your customized character as an "OCDataString" and share it with your friends.
    • You can save your customized session as a "SessionURL" and share it with your friends!
  • You can look for rare sessions, and filter based on what you're looking for.
  • You can view other people's customized Original Characters.
  • You can try to solve a riddle.
  • You can join a community.
