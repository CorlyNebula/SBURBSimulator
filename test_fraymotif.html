Tier: Aspect1:



A procedural Fraymotif will have 1 or more aspects associated with it. The player gaining the fraymotif will ALWAYS have their aspect in the fraymotif. Up to 3 aspects are planned per fraymotif, but more could be supported. Each aspect will add an effect (Life would modify hp, for example), do damage to an enemy, or cause a status effect. Fraymotif name is randomized based on aspect or (if only one aspect), tier. Fraymotif tier is a rough estimate of how powerful it is. Tier 1 is available right away, 2 after beating the denizen, and 3 upon reaching GodTier.

Also, maybe will make fraymotifs be colored based on what aspect made the word. So, fraymotifs are rainbow.

Fraymotif names will HILARIOUSLY be unrelated to their effects. "Endless Apocalypse" might just buff the players time/doom related stats, for example, instead of being an epic attack.

Also, fraymotifs should have pre-written (for sprites and low bloods) or procedural (for claspects) flavor text related to what the actual effects of it are.
